North American Industry Classification System (NAICS):

[U.S. Census Bureau]NAICS is the current industry classification that was developed jointly by the U.S., Canada, and Mexico to provide new comparability in statistics about business activity across North America. There is a 2002 version of NAICS and the initial 1997 version of NAICS.

Keywords: industry classification
