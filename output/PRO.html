Statistical Classification of Domestic and Foreign Commodities Exported from the United States (Schedule B):

By the U.S. Census Bureau, the Statistical Classification of Domestic and Foreign Commodities, also known as Schedule B, is a 10-digit identification code assigned to every product exported from the United States. It contains the 9,000 export codes used to identify commodities. Schedule B has the official schedule of commodity classifications to be used by shippers in reporting export shipments from the United States and in compiling the official statistics on exports of merchandise from the United States. The export commodity classification system presented in this schedule reflects the adoption of the Harmonized Commodity Description and Coding System (Harmonized System).

Tags: product codes
